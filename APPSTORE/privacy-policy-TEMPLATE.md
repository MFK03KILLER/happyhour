[Company Name] ("we", "us") operates the Happy Hour and Happy Hour Merchant mobile apps (the "Apps"). This policy explains what we collect and why.

## Information we collect
- **Account info:** name, email address, phone number (to create and secure your account).
- **Location (customer app):** precise location, only while using the app, to show nearby participating venues and distances. You can deny this; the app still works with reduced functionality.
- **Purchases:** subscription status and order/redemption history.
- **Usage & diagnostics:** in-app interactions and crash data, to improve the Apps.
- **Camera (merchant app):** used on-device to scan customer QR codes. We do not store camera images.

## How we use it
To provide the service (membership, deals, QR redemption), secure accounts, process payments through our payment providers, provide support, and improve the Apps. We do **not** use your data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

## Sharing
We share data only with service providers that help us operate (e.g. hosting, payment processing, sign-in providers such as Apple and Google) under contract, and when required by law.

## Data retention & deletion
You can delete your account and associated personal data at any time from within the app (Profile → Delete Account). You may also contact us at [support email].

## Sign in with Apple / Google
If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a verified identifier and (if you allow) your name/email to create your account.

## Children
The Apps are not directed to children under 13.
